Crowbar room

Hello. Welcome to the crowbar room. Below are accounts of various employees at Black Mesa on the day they created the fall of man.

Doctor Jeffery Tuckerstein

We didn't know what was going on. All we knew was Smithers had some sort of blob-spider on his head, and it was tranforming him into an undiscovered creature of some kind. it was a horrifying sight to see. I was one of the few from room A-C: 14 to survive. I heard. A-D: 14 didn't make it, and that room had around 15 people in it!

Doctor Tim Smith

I thought it was a pipe bursting.

Doctor Matthew Millie Johnson

I was roaming the corridor just outside of the test chamber... you know, the ones with military computers lining the walls? I was asked to turn knob T-20, I think. I never made it to that knob. I was just past the column that houses U-9 [a button], waving at Doctor Cameron, when all of a sudden one of the computers blows up and catches fire. A laser, whcich I assume came from one of the electric valves in the computer bursts out from the metal and explodes him! There was blood everywhere...

Officer Alphred Gunn

I was trying to get some Skittles out of the vending machine. I lost my leg to an explosion coming from a vent near the floor.

Doctor Juncubre

I knew exacty what was going on. Coomer KNEW the test wouldn't work. He did it anyway. It costed so many lives. I hate him.

Rise and shine, Mister Freeman. Rise and... shine...
